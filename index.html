Introduction

I find it hard to be generalized into one of the usual categories. My interests span from online games with friends around the country to baking or cooking some great food for people around me. Where I'm From

I grew up in Wisconsin just outside of Milwaukee so I'm a born and bred Cheesehead. However I attend college out east in Rochester, New York at the Rochester Institute of Technology which has given me a glimpse of the differences between the midwest and the east coast.

More About Me

Why web development?

I first messed around with web development in high school, however a lack of resources from the school and an inexperienced teacher lead me to lose interest initially. Then after some time in college, I ended up working on a project and helping out with the design work for our front end and really began to enjoy myself. After that semester I spent the summer focusing on expanding my experience with web development and learning industry best practices through a course at Thinkful. Since then I've begun to look at other ways to expand my skillset and build out my portfolio.

What's your dream job?

My absolute dream job would involve bringing together two things that I love which are web development and tasting various types of beer. Having worked for MillerCoors I've spent a lot of time around beer and have a growing interest in brewing and the creation of different types of beer. Finding a way to blend the two together would have to be the most interesting and fun job around.
